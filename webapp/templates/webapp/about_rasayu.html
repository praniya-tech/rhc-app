Rasayu Cancer Clinic

Rasayu Cancer Clinic (RCC) is a unique pioneering venture which offers comprehensive solutions to cancer patients. It is a one-of-its-kind cancer care center which provides evidence based highly advanced therapies which are based on principles of the Indian System of Medicine- Ayurveda (ISM). Ayurved is rich in unique fundamentals about the concept of health, the pathogenesis of a disease and its treatments through manufacturing of highly potent and efficacious pharmaceutical drugs. Dyumna Women's Clinic

Dyumna women's clinic (DWC) is focused on preventive as well as curative aspects pertaining to women's health. This is the brainchild of Dr. Vineeta Bendale under the umbrella of Rasayu Ayurved Clinics.

Dr. Bendale has ensured that the manufacturing processes and ingredients strictly adhere to original Ayurveda literature and its compliance, ensuring authentic drugs. Rasayani Biologics (Fundamental Drug Research Centre) - DSIR approved lab

An innovation driven biotech integrating wisdom of Ayurveda with modern day research and regulatory compliance, RBPL was founded in 2007 in Pune. It specializes at developing proprietary oral therapeutics for oncology and chronic diseases. The proprietary products contain noble metals and base elements processed using a patented green technology. RBPL has a well equipped research facility, recognized by Department of Science and Industrial Research (DSIR) and funded by the Department of Biotechnology (DBT), Govt. of India and a pilot manufacturing facility for active Pharmaceutical ingredients (API) approved by Indian FDA.
