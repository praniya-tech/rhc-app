Āyurveda is an ancient Indian science practiced since millenia. Āyurveda inherently focuses on preventive healthcare. This app attempts to do a high level health and risk assessment through interactive questionnaires and tracking and give personalised and preventive healthcare recommendations.

Health (svāsthya) assessment

Āyurveda classifies a person we generally consider to be "healthy" further as disease-free (nirogī) and healthy (svastha). A svastha person is more likely to enjoy a happier, longer and fulfilling life. The app attempts to do a high level health assessment of the user based on a health assessment questionnaire. The app provides recommendations to improve or maintain a good health.

Daily, seasonal routine (dinacaryā, ṛtucaryā)

The life style as recommended by Āyurveda is quite easy to follow. In India, the general daily and seasonal routines and regimens prescribed by Āyurveda were quite well known and practiced by the people and was traditionally passed on through generations. However, due to various factors this knowledge and practice has gradually waned. This app aims to enable users to inculcate this practice and follow it throughout their life.

Health risk assessment and mitigation

Based on a person's quality of tissue (dhātu sāratā), family history, lifestyle, and other factors, Āyurveda enables assessing health risks and accordingly recommending preventive measures for the same.
