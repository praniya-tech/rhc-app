{% blocktrans %} Recommendation for Kapha prakṛti - śiśir ṛtu (mid-January to mid-March) {% endblocktrans %}

{% blocktrans %}
  • Cereals – barley, corn, millet, rye, buckwheat. Cereals in limited quantiy - oat, rice, wheat.
  • Pulses – Moog daal, red lentil. Pulses in limited quantity - kidney beans, chickpeas, pinto, Lima, Soy and split peas.
  • Oils – flaxseed, sunflower oil, ghee.
  • Milk products – low fat milk, yogurt or ghee made of low fat milk, goat’s milk, sheep’s milk, soya milk. Milk should be taken along with teaspoon of turmeric or ginger which will reduce kapha increasing quality of milk. Dairy products are known to increase kapha so better avoid them or consume low fat, calorie version.
  • Vegetables – sweet bitter veggies like asparagus, cabbage, carrot, garlic, radishes, onion, bean sprouts, green beans, peppers, mushrooms, beetroot, etc.
  • Fruits – apple, pear, watermelon, pomegranate, apricot, cranberries. To be consumed in less quantity: bananas, pineapple, oranges, coconut, dates.
  • Drinks - herbal tea, teas with cinnamon, ginger and bitter substances, soups are best pacifying kapha.
  • Meat - chiken, turkey, eggs, prawns.
  • Seed and dry fruits - almond, chia seeds, pumpkin seed, sunflower seeds.
  • Sweeteners - except for honey other sweeteners are better avoided.
  • Exercise – Kapha dominant people have highest endurance of all the doshas and can perform any exercise for long period of time. It's best that kapha person to exercise in the kapha time (early morning) of day.
  • Ritucharya – kapha increases in shirish ritu so kapha person avoid eating too sweet, fatty, heavy meals during this season. Engage in moderate exercise and follow shishir ritu charya.
